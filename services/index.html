01 / Consulting

Expert direction.
You stay hands-on.

For self-managing hosts, new property owners, and second-home owners setting up for the first time. One-off engagements — no ongoing commitment.

Enquire about consulting →

What's included

🔍

Listing audit

Full review of an existing Airbnb listing: title, description, photo assessment, pricing, amenities, house rules, calendar settings. Written report with specific recommendations. Follow-up included.

🚀

New property setup

For owners starting from scratch. Listing creation, copywriting, Pricelabs pricing configuration, smart lock setup, welcome guide, automated messaging. Any new listing managed through me will also be added to bloomcottages.co.uk.

📊

Pricing strategy review

Standalone service. Analysis of current pricing against local Norfolk comparables using Pricelabs data. Written recommendations on nightly rates, minimum stays, seasonal pricing, and gap-fill strategy.

💬

1-to-1 consulting session

In-person for PE31 area; remote video call for wider catchment. 60–90 minutes, agenda set in advance. For hosts with a specific problem or decision to make.
