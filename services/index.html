Our Services

Three ways to work together

From a one-off listing audit to full, hands-off management. Every service is Airbnb-only. No Booking.com. No spreading thin.

01 / Consulting

Expert direction. You stay hands-on.

For self-managing hosts, new property owners, and second-home owners setting up for the first time. One-off engagements — no ongoing commitment.

search

Listing Audit

Full review of an existing Airbnb listing — title, description, photos, pricing, amenities, house rules, calendar settings. Written report with specific recommendations. Follow-up included.

rocket_launch

New Property Setup

For owners starting from scratch. Listing creation, copywriting, Pricelabs pricing configuration, smart lock setup, welcome guide, and automated messaging. New listings are also added to bloomcottages.co.uk.

monitoring

Pricing Strategy Review

Standalone service. Analysis of current pricing against local Norfolk comparables using Pricelabs data. Written recommendations on nightly rates, minimum stays, seasonal pricing, and gap-fill strategy.

forum

1-to-1 Consulting Session

60–90 minute session, agenda set in advance. In-person for the PE31 area; remote video call for wider catchment. For hosts with a specific problem or decision to make.

Process

What happens after you enquire

1

You get in touch

Fill in the contact form with the basics about your property.

2

We reply within 24 hours

Usually with a couple of questions or an initial view on your property.
