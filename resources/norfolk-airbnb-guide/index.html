The Norfolk Airbnb
Owner's Guide

Ten chapters on what Norfolk property owners need to know — whether you're considering Airbnb for the first time, or your existing listing isn't performing as expected. Written specifically for the West and North Norfolk market.

What's inside

  1. Is your Norfolk property right for Airbnb? Property types, what works in this market, PE31 and North Norfolk specifics
  2. Second homes and Airbnb — covering costs, council tax, and what realistic income looks like
  3. What you need before you list — legal requirements, safety certifications, insurance
  4. How Airbnb's algorithm actually works — and why it matters from day one
  5. Why Airbnb-only beats multi-platform — the compounding effect explained
  6. Pricing your Norfolk property — seasonal demand, minimum stays, PE31 vs Burnham Market vs North Norfolk
  7. The Superhost standard — what it is, what it requires, why it changes everything
  8. Setting up for hands-off hosting — technology, automation, cleaning systems
  9. Common mistakes Norfolk hosts make — and how to avoid them
  10. When to get help — what a manager or consultant does that you can't do at scale

Who wrote it

Daniel runs BloomBNB from Dersingham in PE31. He manages Airbnb properties in West and North Norfolk and has been a Superhost long enough to know what actually works in this market. The guide is written from that experience — not from generic Airbnb hosting advice.
